Why Plateio

Your current system is costing you daily.

Here's what running a restaurant looks like today

Without Plateio

The daily reality for most restaurants

Menu changes take days & cost money

Call the printer, wait 3–5 days, pay $200+ every time. Wrong prices stay live until new menus arrive.

No idea who your regulars are

Guests walk in and out — zero record of who they are, how often they visit, or what they like.

Marketing is manual and expensive

Post on Instagram and hope. No targeted emails, no automated campaigns, no win-back for lapsed guests.

Kitchen runs on paper tickets & shouting

Tickets get wet, lost, or misread. Expo chefs coordinate stations by yelling across the kitchen.

Losses only visible at month-end

Food cost variance and missed upsells only appear in last month's P&L — too late to fix anything.

Upselling depends entirely on staff

Great servers upsell well. Short-staffed Tuesday? That revenue is just gone with no fallback.

With Plateio

What your operation looks like from day one

Menu live in seconds, not days

Change a price, add a special, 86 an item — updates go live instantly across all tables the moment you hit save. Zero print cost.

Every guest has a profile from visit one

Plateio automatically builds a CRM record — visits, spend, preferences, birthdays — with no manual entry ever needed.

Marketing runs itself, every day

Win-back emails fire when a regular goes quiet. Birthday rewards send automatically. Promos reach the right guests at the right time.

Kitchen runs on live digital screens

Orders route to the right station instantly. Color-coded timers replace paper chaos. Every prep time tracked and optimised.

Revenue gaps visible in real time

Live dashboards show food cost variance, RevPASH, and upsell rate while you can still act on it — not a month later.

Upsells happen on every single order

Smart menu logic suggests add-ons and pairings at point of ordering — consistent on every cover, every shift, regardless of staff.
